Usage:

	$ python3 ji-helper.py <command> <arguments>

Commands:

	--- min ---

	minimizes one ratio, i.e 10/8 becomes 5/4

	--- force ---

	forces one ratio into the octave range, i.e 9/4 becomes 9/8

	--- mult ---

	multiplies two ratios and minimizes them

	--- div ---

	divides two ratios and minimizes them
